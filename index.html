About
01

The operator behind the numbers.

At 22, I started building commercially before I had a job title for it. In 2020 I founded Perfect Entry, a digital trading education and analytics platform, from a simple idea with no funding and no team. Over the following years I built it into a business generating close to £100k a month at peak, handling every function myself: acquisition, pricing, content, retention, onboarding, and analytics. Getting there meant learning what most people only read about.

That experience gave me something most early-career candidates do not have: a real understanding of what it takes to grow a business. Not the theory, but the infrastructure underneath it. The systems, the tracking, the decisions about where to spend time and where not to. I think in inputs and outputs, and I build processes that hold up when things get messy.

Since then I have taken that mindset into other environments, working in medtech distribution, precision engineering, and commercial partnerships. The pattern is consistent: I find where growth is being left on the table, build the structure to capture it, and execute. I am finishing a BSc Economics at the University of Essex in 2026, focused on behavioural finance, market structure, and quantitative analysis.

"Build structured systems that allow teams to execute consistently. Combine analytical decision-making with entrepreneurial execution to drive growth that actually holds."
